Who We Are

A Community Rooted in Faith & Love

AFM Koinonia Centre is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church family passionate about authentic fellowship with God and with one another. We believe that the church is not just a building, but a living community of believers walking together in grace.


Whether you're seeking answers, looking for a spiritual home, or simply curious, you are welcome here. Come as you are you belong with us.
